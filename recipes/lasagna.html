Lasagna

Description

This is an easy lasagna recipe that you can do at home. It is a delicious and tasty dish that is loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the tomato sauce
  2. Grate the Mozarella cheese
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Bake the lasagna
