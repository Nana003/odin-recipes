Salmon

Description

This is an easy baked salmon recipe that use few ingredients. Despite it's simplicity, it is a delicious and tasty dish that can easily be loved by anyone!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the salmon in a baking pan
  2. Add the olive oil, salt, garlic and lemon
  3. Mix it all and put it on the salmon
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Bake the salmon
